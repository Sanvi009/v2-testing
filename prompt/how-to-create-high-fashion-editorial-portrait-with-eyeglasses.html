How to Create a High-Fashion Editorial Portrait with Eyeglasses

Published: 2026-03-20
Author: promptgalary

Learn how to generate a striking, avant-garde high-fashion portrait using AI. This prompt creates a sleek, editorial-style upper-body shot with luxury styling, statement makeup, eyeglasses, and dramatic studio lighting against a clean pure white background. Perfect for beauty campaigns, fashion portfolios, and stylized character design.

How to Use This Prompt

To customize this editorial prompt, you can easily adapt the styling and environment to fit your vision. Start by modifying the accessories; change 'wearing eyeglasses' to 'wearing oversized sunglasses' or 'wearing a pearl choker'. You can adjust the beauty details by swapping 'sleek hair tucked behind ears' for 'voluminous bouncy curls' and 'glossy skin' for 'matte porcelain skin'. To completely change the mood, replace the 'clean pure white background' with a 'dark moody textured grey background' or a 'vibrant neon pink studio backdrop'. Finally, experiment with the fashion by changing 'avant-garde styling' to 'minimalist tailored suit' or 'vintage 1990s streetwear'.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I make sure the AI uses my exact face?
    To maintain your facial identity, you must use a platform that supports image prompting or character reference features. In Midjourney, utilize the `--cref` parameter followed by the URL of your uploaded image. Ensure your source image has clear, even lighting on your face for the AI to analyze your features accurately.
  • Can I remove the eyeglasses from the prompt entirely?
    Yes, absolutely. If you want a standard editorial portrait without eyewear, simply delete the phrase 'wearing eyeglasses' from the prompt. You can leave that space empty, or replace it with another accessory like 'wearing large statement earrings'.
  • How do I change the pure white background to a specific color?
    To change the background color, locate the phrase 'clean pure white background' within the prompt text. Replace 'pure white' with your desired color or texture, such as 'clean pure pastel pink background', 'solid matte black background', or 'vibrant emerald green background'.
