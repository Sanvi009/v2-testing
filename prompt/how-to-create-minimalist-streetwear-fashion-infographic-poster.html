How to Create a Minimalist Streetwear Fashion Infographic Poster

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-03-10
Author: promptgalary

Learn how to transform your photo into an ultra-realistic, Korean or Japanese style streetwear fashion infographic poster. This AI prompt creates a clean, full-length lookbook layout complete with custom text, clothing annotations, and product callouts.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

Transform this image  into a 64K DSLR-quality shot resolution ultra-hyperrealistic a clean minimalist streetwear fashion infographic full-length poster featuring a young male model (same face as the uploaded image) styled in a modern casual outfit titled “[Add Text]” The composition should resemble a Korean/Japanese fashion catalog layout with product callouts and annotations pointing to each clothing item.ar 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

To customize this prompt for your own lookbook: 1. Replace '[Add Text]' with your desired poster title or brand name. 2. Change 'modern casual outfit' to a specific style like 'dark techwear', 'vintage 90s grunge', or 'smart casual'. 3. Switch 'Korean/Japanese fashion catalog' to another aesthetic, such as 'London streetwear magazine' or 'Scandinavian minimalist lookbook'. 4. Adjust the aspect ratio by changing 'ar 9:16' to 'ar 3:4' for standard portrait or 'ar 16:9' for a landscape website banner.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I change the title of the poster?
    Simply replace the placeholder '[Add Text]' in the prompt with your desired phrase. For example, you can write 'Spring Collection 2026' or 'Daily Streetwear Fit'. Advanced AI models like Midjourney v6 and DALL-E 3 are excellent at rendering specific text.
  • What AI tool is best for generating text and layouts?
    Midjourney v6 is highly recommended for this prompt as it handles text generation, magazine layouts, and hyper-realistic photographic rendering exceptionally well. DALL-E 3 is also a great alternative for strict adherence to text and infographic callouts.
  • How can I ensure the clothing annotations are accurate?
    While AI can generate the visual layout of annotations and lines, it may invent the text for the clothing items (like 'Cotton Tee' or 'Cargo Pants'). For complete accuracy, you can generate a clean layout with placeholder lines and add your own specific text in an editing program like Photoshop or Canva.
