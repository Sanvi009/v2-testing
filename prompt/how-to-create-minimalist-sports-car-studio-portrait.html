How to Use This Prompt

Step 1: Upload a clear reference image of your face to use for the identity replacement. Step 2: Modify the car model by changing 'porche 911 matte black' to your favorite vehicle, such as 'Ferrari F40 red' or 'matte grey Lamborghini'. Step 3: Update the outfit by replacing the 'glossy black tracksuit' and 'metallic silver sneakers' with your desired clothing, like 'a tailored charcoal suit' or 'high-end streetwear'. Step 4: Change the camera perspective from 'high-angle' to 'low-angle' or 'eye-level' to drastically alter the composition. Step 5: Adjust the studio background from 'plain white' to 'seamless dark grey' or 'neon-lit' to shift the overall mood.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I change the car featured in the background?

  • Can I change the camera angle for this portrait?
    Yes. The prompt currently specifies a 'high-angle photograph'. You can alter the composition entirely by changing this to 'low-angle photograph', 'eye-level shot', or 'overhead drone perspective'.
  • How do I make the studio lighting look more dramatic?
    To achieve a moodier, dramatic look, change the lighting description from 'soft and even, reducing harsh shadows' to something like 'harsh directional spotlighting with deep, sharp shadows' or 'moody cinematic lighting'.
