Glowing Stone App Icons

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2025-09-20
Author: promptgalary

Generated Images

The Prompt 

Create a set of 9 iPhone app icons in the following style:

Visual Style: Rounded square icons with a rugged stone texture, carved with cracks that emit a bright purple glow (neon violet with soft magenta highlights).Lighting & Background: Place each icon on a pure white background so the icons appear bright, clear, and luminous. The purple glow should softly radiate outward, making the icons look as if they are emitting light.

Material: Polished yet weathered stone with realistic cracks, glowing runes, and smooth highlights.

Consistency: All icons should match the same bright glowing style.
Icons to include:

Safari

Messages

Camera

Instagram

YouTube

TikTok

Spotify

Mail

Settings
The overall feel should be clean, modern, and magical — dark stone contrasted against a white background, with radiant purple energy shining through.

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, integrate it into app icon redesigns, UI kits, or magical branding for tech and fantasy apps. Preserve "set of 9 iPhone app icons," "rugged stone texture," "bright purple glow," and "clean, modern, and magical" feel for consistency. Do not change the rounded square or white background. Replacing apps with "Facebook" could produce social icons, leading to radiant energy, while original yields glowing runes with dark stone contrasts.
