How to Create a Gritty Top-Down Warehouse Portrait with Scattered Money

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-03-18
Author: promptgalary

Learn how to generate a hyper-realistic, medium shot from a high top-down angle, featuring a specific subject sitting in a dimly lit, dark warehouse. This prompt emphasizes gritty details, cold cinematic lighting, and a floor completely covered in scattered US dollar bills, creating a secure and slightly tense atmosphere perfect for industrial or wealth-themed concept art.

How to Use This Prompt

To customize this prompt, try altering the environment by replacing 'dark warehouse' with a 'luxury bank vault' or 'abandoned subway tunnel.' You can switch the subject to a different person or adjust the camera perspective from 'high top-down angle' to 'eye-level shot' for varied compositions. Modify the clothing by changing 'olive-green plush fleece sweater' to a 'black tailored suit' or 'modern streetwear' with different knitted graphics. For different lighting effects, experiment with 'warm security lighting' or 'harsh red emergency lights' to shift the atmospheric mood. You can also add specific objects like weapons or modify the type of scattered currency to include multiple international banknotes.
