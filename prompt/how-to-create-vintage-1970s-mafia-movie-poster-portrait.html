How to Create a Vintage 1970s Mafia Movie Poster Portrait

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-03-18
Author: promptgalary

Learn how to transform your photo into an iconic, 1970s-inspired mafia movie poster. This hyperrealistic prompt generates a dramatic chiaroscuro portrait featuring a classic tuxedo, a red rose lapel, and vintage marionette puppet string typography, perfect for a personalized cinematic masterpiece.

The Prompt 

Transform this uploaded image into a 64K DSLR-quality shot resolution ultra-hyperrealistic cinematic movie poster in the exact style of DO FOLLOW using the uploaded image of the user as the main subject (same face as the as the uploaded image). The composition should feature a dark, minimalist black background. On the left side of the frame, place a close-up portrait of the uploaded person with a stern, solemn expression. The person should be dressed in a black tuxedo, white shirt, and black bow tie, with a single red rose pinned to the lapel. The face should be partially shadowed, illuminated only from one side with dramatic, high-contrast lighting (chiaroscuro effect), emphasizing the eyes, cheeks, and mouth. The right side of the image should be mostly empty black space, reserved for text. Add the movie title “[ADD TEXT]” in large, bold, white serif letters — matching the exact  style of the original logo. Above the title, include a stylized white puppet hand holding marionette strings, giving the appearance of controlling the text below. The overall atmosphere should be dark, elegant, and powerful, evoking a sense of authority, secrecy, and legacy. Maintain a minimal color palette of black, white, and red, with extremely high contrast between light and shadow. The design should look cinematic, vintage, and 1970s-inspired — resembling an iconic mafia film poster  Style keywords: 1970s film poster, high contrast noir lighting, minimalist design, dramatic portrait, mafia aesthetic, black background, vintage typography, classic Hollywood lighting, matte finish, elegant composition, graphic poster art, serif logo font, red rose accent. Camera framing: tight head and shoulders portrait on the left; centered lighting; dark background fading to black on the right side.  Color grading: deep blacks, bright whites, muted midtones, red accent. Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 2:1 (widescreen movie poster format). Important: Use the uploaded image as the person in the poster. No additional text, no background elements, no other people — focus entirely on the single figure, the puppet-hand graphic, and the title text. Octane render, Ultra-hyperrealistic & Unreal Engine 5 AR 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

To customize this poster, replace '[ADD TEXT]' with your own title or name to personalize the typography. You can change the outfit by swapping 'black tuxedo, white shirt, and black bow tie' with 'pinstripe suit' or 'trench coat'. If you want a different accent color, change the 'red rose' to a 'white carnation' or a 'gold lapel pin'. Finally, adjust the aspect ratio parameter at the very end of the prompt (ar 9:16 for a vertical poster, or ar 16:9 for a desktop wallpaper) depending on your needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get the text generation to work perfectly?
    To ensure the text '[ADD TEXT]' renders correctly, use an AI model specifically trained for typography, such as Midjourney v6 or DALL-E 3. Keep your desired title short (1-3 words) and enclose it in quotation marks within the prompt for the best results.
  • What if my generated image looks too bright?
    This prompt relies heavily on 'chiaroscuro' and 'high contrast noir lighting'. If your result is too bright, ensure the reference photo you upload isn't overly illuminated or flat. You can also add words like 'pitch black shadows' or 'underexposed' to force the AI to darken the composition.
  • Why does the prompt mention both widescreen and AR 9:16?
    The prompt describes the internal composition of a widescreen poster, but uses the aspect ratio parameter 'ar 9:16' at the end to force a vertical, mobile-friendly format. If you want a horizontal, cinematic desktop wallpaper, simply change 'AR 9:16' to 'AR 16:9' at the very end of the prompt.
