Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I ensure the Ottoman architecture is accurately represented?
    To achieve accurate Ottoman architecture, be specific about location details in your prompt. Istanbul features distinct architectural elements like domes, minarets, Iznik tilework, and arched windows. You can mention particular landmarks such as 'Süleymaniye Mosque,' 'Topkapi Palace,' or 'Dolmabahçe Palace' for reference. The AI will draw from its training data on these famous structures. Including terms like 'elegant Ottoman architecture with intricate tile details and arched columns' helps guide the architectural style.
  • What does 'DSLR 85mm' add to the image quality?
    DSLR 85mm refers to using an 85mm prime lens on a professional DSLR camera, which is a classic focal length for portraits. It creates a natural perspective with minimal distortion, beautiful background compression, and excellent subject isolation. When included in an AI prompt, it signals the desired camera equipment and lens characteristics, resulting in professional portrait photography qualities including accurate proportions, pleasing background blur (bokeh), and that distinctive high-end fashion editorial look.
  • How can I achieve the 'warm golden sunset' lighting effect consistently?
    To consistently achieve warm golden sunset lighting, specify the time of day and quality of light. 'Golden hour' refers to the first hour after sunrise or last hour before sunset when light is warm and soft. You can enhance this by adding terms like 'long soft shadows,' 'warm amber highlights,' 'sun rays filtering through architecture,' or 'golden backlight with lens flare.' The 16K resolution and cinematic color grading terms also help maintain the high-quality, warm-toned aesthetic throughout the image generation.
