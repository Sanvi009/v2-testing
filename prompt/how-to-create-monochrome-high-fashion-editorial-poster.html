How to Use This Prompt

To customize this high-fashion editorial prompt, start by changing the color theme: swap 'pure red' and 'monochrome red outfit' for 'deep royal blue' or 'stark black'. You can also change the custom text by replacing 'Promptgalary' with your own name or brand. Adjust the wardrobe by changing 'oversized hoodie, joggers, sneakers' to 'tailored suit' or 'avant-garde gown'. Finally, switch out the 'modern designer chair' for a 'vintage leather armchair' or 'minimalist concrete block' to alter the set design.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I change the text on the poster?
    You can easily customize the text by changing the word 'Promptgalary' in the prompt to your own name, brand, or desired phrase. Be sure to keep the single quotes around your text so the AI knows exactly what to spell out.
  • Can I use this prompt to generate a different color theme?
    Absolutely. This prompt is highly modular. To change the theme, replace the word 'red' in 'monochrome red outfit' and 'pure red background' with any color you prefer, such as 'emerald green', 'lavender', or 'charcoal grey'.
  • Which AI generator works best for custom typography?
    Midjourney v6 and DALL-E 3 are currently the industry leaders for generating legible, accurate typography within images. Midjourney v6 is particularly strong at blending text seamlessly into high-fashion, magazine-style layouts.
