Upload a clear photo of yourself. Copy and paste the entire prompt. The AI will generate a vertical (9:16), film-style candid portrait using your likeness, placing you in the described night scene with the correct clothing and bag type for your gender.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'candid' and 'unaware of the camera' mean for the AI?
    It instructs the AI to generate a natural, relaxed pose where the subject isn't looking directly at the viewer or posing deliberately. This creates a more authentic, street-photography feel.
  • How does the AI handle 'high-angle' and 'shot from an upper floor'?
    These terms describe the camera's perspective. The AI will position the virtual camera above and looking down at the subject, making the person appear smaller within the frame and showing more of the ground/pavement around them.
  • What if I don't specify a bag type?
    The prompt instructs to 'add the bag type according to gender.' Based on common associations, the AI will likely choose a tote or crossbody bag for a female subject and a messenger bag or backpack for a male subject, but specifying it in your own copy of the prompt gives you more control.
