How to Use This Prompt

To customize this prompt, try altering the clothing by replacing 'elegant suit outfit' with 'streetwear apparel' or 'haute couture gown'. You can adjust the camera angle by changing 'cinematic low-angle perspective' to 'eye-level shot' or 'dutch angle'. For different lighting effects, modify 'dramatic rim lighting' to 'soft pastel lighting' or 'neon cyberpunk glow'. Change the background from 'minimalist solid background' to 'abstract geometric background' to perfectly fit your specific poster design needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I maintain the exact facial likeness of my original photo?
    To preserve identity, use consistent, high-quality reference images. If using an image-to-image workflow, keep your denoise strength relatively low so the AI respects the original bone structure. In Midjourney, utilize the character reference parameter for the best results.
  • Can I use this prompt in Midjourney or Stable Diffusion?
    Yes, this prompt is highly effective in both. For Midjourney, use image prompts or the cref feature to blend the photo with the style. For Stable Diffusion, pairing this prompt with a ControlNet module will help lock in the exact pose and facial features while applying the vector aesthetic.
  • How do I change the color palette of the vector illustration?
    You can easily control the colors by adding specific color keywords into the prompt. For example, insert phrases like 'monochrome palette', 'neon pink and blue gradients', or 'warm autumn colors' near the shading and background descriptions to completely shift the mood.
