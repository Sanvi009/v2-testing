Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why does this prompt include weights and negative phrasing?
    This prompt is formatted specifically for AI engines that support complex syntax, like Stable Diffusion. The numbers (e.g., :1.3) tell the AI to prioritize certain keywords over others. The negative phrases at the bottom prevent the AI from generating unwanted artifacts like bad anatomy or plastic-looking skin.
  • How do I successfully change the custom signature in the corner?
    To change the signature, you need to modify two parts of the prompt. First, find `Text "by Hamann"` and change it to your name. Second, look for the spelling instruction `Latin characters b-y-space-H-a-m-a-n-n` and update it to spell out your new text letter by letter. This helps the AI render the typography accurately.
  • Can I use this prompt in Midjourney?
    Yes, you can use this in Midjourney! However, Midjourney doesn't process weight formatting like `(keyword:1.5)` the same way Stable Diffusion does. While it will still generate a stunning image, you may want to remove the parentheses and numbers, and move the negative prompts to an `--no` parameter at the end of your prompt.
