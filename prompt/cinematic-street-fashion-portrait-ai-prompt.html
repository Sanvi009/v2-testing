Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need to upload a photo to use this prompt?
    For the most accurate personalization, yes. The prompt is designed to work with an uploaded reference image to copy your facial features. However, you can still use it without one to generate a generic model.
  • Which AI image generator works best for this prompt?
    Midjourney and DALL-E 3 are excellent for this style due to their strong understanding of photographic styles and detailed textures. Stable Diffusion can also produce great results, especially with a photorealistic model checkpoint.
  • How do I get the clothing details right?
    The prompt is very specific. If the AI misses a detail like 'hanging cords' or 'patchwork', try rephrasing that part or adding weight to those keywords (e.g., putting them in quotes or using '::' emphasis in some tools).
