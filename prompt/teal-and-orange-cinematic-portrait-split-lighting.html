Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is teal and orange color grading?
    It's a popular cinematic color style where skin tones are shifted toward orange and shadows/backgrounds toward teal (blue-green), creating strong visual contrast.
  • How do I ensure the AI keeps the same facial identity?
    Use the 'image prompt' or 'reference image' feature in your AI tool. Upload a clear, front-facing photo of the person. The prompt includes 'exact same facial and physical identity' to reinforce this.
  • What is split lighting?
    Split lighting is a technique where one half of the face is lit and the other is in shadow. This prompt specifies a 'hard vertical beam of warm orange light' to create that effect.
