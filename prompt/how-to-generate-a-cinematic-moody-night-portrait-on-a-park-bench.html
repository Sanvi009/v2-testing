Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I ensure the AI generates the 'exact same face' consistently?
    To achieve consistent facial identity, use an AI image generator that supports 'character reference' features, like Midjourney's '--cref' or Stable Diffusion's reference/embedding techniques. First, generate or upload an image of the face you want to use as a reference. Then, in your prompt, include that reference image URL or token along with the descriptive prompt. The phrase 'exact same face' in your prompt helps reinforce this instruction to the AI.
  • What do '8k ultra image' and 'ar 9:16' mean in a prompt?
    '8k ultra image' is a quality descriptor that instructs the AI to generate a highly detailed, sharp, and high-resolution image. 'ar 9:16' specifies the aspect ratio of the output, meaning the image will be 9 units tall for every 16 units wide, which is a standard portrait orientation for mobile screens and social media stories.
  • How can I fine-tune the 'moody and lonely' atmosphere in the generated image?
    The atmosphere is heavily influenced by lighting and color. To make it more lonely, emphasize words like 'isolated,' 'alone,' 'vast empty space,' or 'quiet.' To fine-tune the mood, adjust the lighting descriptors: 'harsh shadows' for a more dramatic feel, 'soft, diffused light' for a gentler sadness, or 'cold blue tones' for a more somber mood. You can also add environmental elements like 'fog,' 'rain,' or 'falling leaves' to enhance the emotional impact.
