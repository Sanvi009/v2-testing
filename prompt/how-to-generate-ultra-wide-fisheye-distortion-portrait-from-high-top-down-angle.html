How to Generate an Ultra-Wide Fisheye Distortion Portrait from a High Top-Down Angle

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-03-18
Author: promptgalary

Discover how to transform a simple photo into a striking ultra-wide fisheye portrait with extreme top-down perspective distortion. This prompt creates a unique, distorted look with oversized sunglasses, a Nike cap, and a color-block puffer jacket against a bold terracotta background, perfect for a modern editorial fashion poster aesthetic.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

Convert uploaded photo into ultra-wide 10mm fisheye portrait from dramatic high top-down angle with strong perspective distortion (larger head, compressed body). Add black oversized RAY-BAN transparent frame sunglasses and Nike logo with white cap, right hand adjusting cap, confident upward gaze. Color-block puffer jacket (burnt orange/cream/brown), white tee, white Nike sneakers. Bold terracotta studio background, smooth gradient, high contrast, sharp editorial lighting, 8k. ar 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

This prompt offers high customization for dramatic portraiture. You can easily adjust the perspective by changing '10mm fisheye' to 'ultra-wide' or 'normal lens' while keeping the angle. Modify the added accessories by replacing 'black RAY-BAN transparent frame sunglasses' with 'modern rimless goggles' or 'vintage round glasses'. Swap the clothing completely, changing 'color-block puffer jacket' to a 'tailored trench coat' or 'denim jacket'. You can also change the 'Nike logo' to another brand or remove it. Finally, replace the 'terracotta studio background' with a 'concrete jungle' or 'neon-lit city street' to shift the entire setting while retaining the powerful high-angle fisheye aesthetic.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I use this prompt to convert any photo into a fisheye portrait?
    Yes, this prompt is designed specifically for image-to-image conversion. You just need to upload your source photo to an AI model that supports this feature (like Stable Diffusion or Midjourney) and paste this text to apply the dramatic high-angle fisheye transformation while preserving the subject's identity.
  • How can I change the background of the portrait without affecting the subject?
    The key is to replace the text describing the background. For example, instead of 'Bold terracotta studio background', you can input 'Busy Tokyo street at night' or 'Concrete skate park'. The AI will keep the subject and perspective while generating a new environment. If you want precise control, you can explore mask generation to separate the subject from the background before applying a new prompt to the background.
  • How do I ensure the perspective distortion doesn't make the subject look unnatural?
    The prompt is structured for controlled exaggeration, where 'strong perspective distortion' specifically means 'larger head, compressed body' for a unique editorial look. To prevent it from looking chaotic, maintain keywords like 'dramatic high top-down angle' and 'sharp editorial lighting' which help the AI interpret the intent. If you find the distortion too strong, try softening the adjectives, changing 'strong' to 'moderate' or specifying an actual lens size like '15mm' for less exaggeration while retaining the top-down feel.
