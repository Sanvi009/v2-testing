About Me

Hi, I’m Rafi — Founder & Lead Researcher

Navigating the immigration process can feel overwhelming. Often, the most valuable and accurate immigration advice is buried in hour-long YouTube videos from legal experts, or hidden behind confusing, dense government jargon.

I created Migrant-Info to solve this exact problem. My mission is to make global mobility accessible by turning complex immigration updates and expert insights into clear, structured, and easy-to-read guides.

How We Create Our Guides

To ensure you get the best information without the headache, we follow a strict editorial process:

Expert Sourcing

We actively monitor content from verified, licensed immigration attorneys and cross-reference it with official government portals (like USCIS and embassy websites).

Analysis & Simplification

We watch the long-form videos and read the heavy policy documents so you don't have to. We take those complex discussions and break them down into step-by-step articles, actionable checklists, and quick summaries.

Strict Attribution

We never claim this legal expertise as our own. Every single article we publish clearly cites its sources and provides direct links back to the original expert's video or the official government page. We want you to be able to verify the facts instantly.

Who This Site Is For

Migrant-Info is built for:

Individuals and families planning a move abroad who need a clear starting point.
Students and professionals trying to understand their visa options.
Anyone looking to save time and avoid simple mistakes by accessing well-organized, research-backed information.

⚠️ Important Disclaimer (Please Read)

I am a dedicated researcher and content curator, not an immigration attorney. Migrant-Info is an independent educational platform.

While we work incredibly hard to summarize expert opinions and policy updates accurately, immigration law is complex and changes rapidly. The content on this website is for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. You should always consult a licensed immigration attorney or check official government websites before making any legal or life-changing decisions.

No attorney-client relationship is formed by using this site.
