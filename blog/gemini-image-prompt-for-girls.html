Mastering AI Portrait Photography for Women

Welcome to our ultimate gallery of AI prompts. Whether you are using Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or DALL-E, generating the perfect high-fashion, cinematic, or street-style portrait requires precise text engineering. In this guide, we provide 16 meticulously crafted prompts designed to generate hyper-realistic, editorial-quality results.

How to Maximize These Prompts:

  • Subject Consistency: Many of these prompts mention using an "uploaded image". If you are using Midjourney, use the --cref (character reference) parameter paired with a URL to your subject to maintain facial identity.
  • Aspect Ratios: We frequently use --ar 9:16 for vertical, social-media-ready portraits (TikTok/Reels). Change this to --ar 16:9 for desktop wallpapers or cinematic wide shots, or --ar 4:5 for standard Instagram posts.
  • Customization is Key: Don't be afraid to swap out keywords! Change a "yellow hoodie" to a "red leather jacket", or an "85mm lens" to a "35mm lens" to drastically alter the vibe.

Below, you'll find the reference images, the exact copy-pasteable prompt, and pro tips for modifying each style to suit your specific creative vision.
