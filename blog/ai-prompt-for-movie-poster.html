How to Use These AI Poster Prompts

Welcome to our curated gallery of premium poster prompts. Whether you are using Midjourney v6, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion, getting the perfect result requires understanding how to communicate with the AI. These prompts are designed as highly structured templates.

To get the most out of these prompts, follow these best practices:

  • Face Swapping: Many of our prompts are designed for personal avatars. If you are using Midjourney, use the --cref parameter combined with a clear image URL. If you are using local Stable Diffusion, utilize tools like ReActor or FaceID nodes.
  • Customize the Variables: Do not just copy and paste! Look for the bracketed text inside the prompts (e.g., [INSERT TEXT] or [CLOTHING COLOR]) and replace them with your own creative choices to make the poster unique.
  • Aspect Ratios: Most of these posters are set to AR 9:16, which is standard for vertical social media formats (like Instagram Reels or TikTok). If you need a cinematic desktop background, simply change the ratio at the end of the prompt to AR 16:9.
