How to Customize These Prompts

While these prompts are ready to use out of the box, they are built to be modular. You can easily adjust them to fit your specific vision:

  • Aspect Ratio: Most of these prompts end with ar 9:16, which creates a vertical image perfect for TikTok or Instagram Reels. To make a widescreen wallpaper, change this to ar 16:9. For a square profile picture, use ar 1:1.
  • Clothing & Branding: In Prompt 18, you will see text like "bold lettering text BRAINLOOM." You can swap "BRAINLOOM" with your own name, brand, or favorite phrase. The AI will seamlessly print it on the clothing fabric.
  • Color Palettes: Prompt 10 utilizes specific HEX codes (e.g., "#7689af"). You can replace these bracketed values with your own brand's specific HEX colors to generate on-brand marketing materials instantly.
